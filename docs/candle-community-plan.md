## The Clipping Team Strategy

The clipping team is Candle's biggest growth lever. Members post content about Candle on CT, earn $CNDL based on views, and the whole ecosystem grows.

### How It Works
- Members submit their CT posts to the Elite Bot
- Views are tracked and verified
- $CNDL is earned at the current CPM rate
- Top clippers appear on the Elite Leaderboard on the community dashboard
- The leaderboard creates competition — competition drives more posting

### Goals
- [ ] 50 active clippers in Month 1
- [ ] 10M total views generated in Month 3
- [ ] Leaderboard updated in real time on the community dashboard
- [ ] Weekly shoutout to the top 3 clippers in `#announcements`
- [ ] Monthly $CNDL bonus to the #1 clipper

### Content Priorities for Clippers
1. Candle launches — be first to post about new tokens launching on Candle
2. $CNDL price milestones — post when the token hits new highs
3. Community wins — screenshot leaderboard moments, ring ascensions
4. @andrew insights — tease Ring 4 content to drive Believer mints
5. Competitor comparisons — why Candle > pump.fun
