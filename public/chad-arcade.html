FAQ
What is $CHAD? +
$CHAD is a Solana memecoin built on Candle TV. Hold $CHAD to access the arcade, play 3 games, and compete for weekly prize pools paid directly in $CHAD.
How do I play the arcade? +
Connect your Phantom wallet and hold at least 100 $CHAD. You'll get instant access to all 3 arcade games: Flappy Beard, Chad Dodge, and Moon or Rug.
How do I win weekly prizes? +
Set a high score in any game, post it on X with $CHAD and #CHADArcade. Top 3 verified scores each week win $100, $50, and $25 in $CHAD sent to your wallet every Sunday.
Is my wallet safe? +
Yes. Read-only connection only — we verify your $CHAD balance to check eligibility. We never request transaction signing and never access your funds.
When are prizes sent? +
Every Sunday after the leaderboard resets at midnight UTC. $CHAD is sent directly to your connected Phantom wallet.
What is the minimum to play? +
100 $CHAD minimum in your Phantom wallet. Your balance is checked each time you connect — no $CHAD, no arcade access.
